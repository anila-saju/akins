About Company

Recognized interior
designer in QATAR

Akins Trading Contracting & Decoration WLL have immense pleasure in introducing ourselves as one of the growing Contractors in the field of Interior Designing/Fit Out in the Qatar. We are an ISO 9001, ISO 14001 & OHSAS18001 certified organization which relentlessly works for the satisfaction of its clients and it’s employees. We are primarily engaged in the enhancement of Interior Design delivered on assignment wise and turnkey basis. The in-house joinery division wasintended to provide support for the Fit Out Works.

We have been fortunate enough to be part of the various prestigious projects for our clients in the Interior Designing and Fitout.
